About Me

My name is Nicholas Hom, and I am a current undergraduate student at UCLA pursuing a degree in Cognitive Science with a Concentration in Computing. My professional interests lie at the intersection of business and technology, and I wanted to use this project not only to further explore the topics discussed throughout this quarter, but also improve my HTML and CSS acumen. I decided to take this class because I have reached the age where I have begun to analyze the way I was raised and wanted to learn more about parenting styles and the reasons we are raised the ways we are.
