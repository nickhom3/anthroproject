About Us




Planning Parenthood exists to education future mothers, fathers, and the greater society of the role of parenting in a child's life. The internet hosts infinite wisdom surrounding the "correct way" to parent without considering the immutable factors within each household, family, geographical area, and culture. A blanket approach to childcare is both inefficient and ineffective in raising children to their fullest potential, and our goal is to help the world understand this.
